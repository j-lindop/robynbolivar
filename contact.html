Before making contact please consider the following:

• If you wish to use private insurance, have you checked to be sure your benefits cover “Registered Psychotherapists”?

• Are you looking for individual, family or couple’s therapy?

• If you identify as non-monogomous or poly, trans or non-binary, queer or questioning your sexuality or gender, please indicate this in your message to me.

• Do you require a specific day of the week, or time of the day? If so please indicate these specifics in your message.

My preference is to have a short (20-30 min) consult call before we start therapy. This phone call is free-of-charge. If you’d like to schedule a consult call please indicate your general availability for the following week.

I am looking forward to hearing from you.
